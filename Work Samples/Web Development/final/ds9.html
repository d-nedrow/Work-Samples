Captain Sisko

Starting the series at the rank of Commander, he is later promoted. His wife was killed in a battle with the Borg, and he initially blames Captain Picard. Now he raises his son, Jake, by himself on Deep Space Nine.

Major Kira

A member of the Bajoran militia, she was a freedom-fighter during the dark and violent Cardassian occupation. Now she is the liason officer to Starfleet, and also serves as Sisko's first officer.

Chief O'Brien

Formerly the transporter chief on the Enterprise, he transfers to DS9 to become the chief engineer. He has a wife Keiko and daugher Molly aboard the station. O'Brien has sour memories of the Cardassians from a previous war.

Doctor Bashir

Initially he is comically eager and annoying, but he settles down to become an excellent officer. Bashir becomes best friends with Chief O'Brien. Later in the series it is revealed that Bashir is genetically engineered, which he and his family have kept secret because it is illegal in the Federation.

Jadzia Dax

Dax is the Science Officer on DS9. She is a Trill, which is a "joined species" which consists of a humanoid host body and a worm-like symbiant living inside the host. The worm-like creature possesses hundreds of years worth of memories and intelligence as it is placed in a new host at the end of the old one's life.

Odo

Odo is a shapeshifter, and he serves as security chief. Unlike on the Enterprise, this position does no include tactical or bridge duties, but is a law enforcement post. He also had this job under Cardassian rule, and is considered very objective by all. He is looking for other members of his species, but they will turn out to be the villainous Founders of the Dominion.

Quark

Quark is a Ferengi bartender, gambler, and criminal who runs a bar and casino on the station. He is constantly being pursued by Odo, but the two have a grudging friendship.

Jake Sisko

The son of Captain Sisko, Jake learns early he doesn't want to follow in his father's footsteps and join Starfleet. Instead, he aspires to become a writer.
