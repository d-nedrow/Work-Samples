Character Notes

Captain Picard

Picard is a philosophical and deeply moralistic Frenchman who is just as likely to solve a problem with diplomacy or a well-timed speech as with action.

Commander Riker

The first officer is called "Number One" by Picard. Riker is more given to action, and is portrayed as a ladies man.

Data

The 2nd officer and operations officer, Data is an android. While he often reminds everyone that he has no feelings, his actions often seem driven by instict and imagination. Data is a tremendous intellectual and a ridiculously valuable officer.

Worf

Worf is the Klingon chief of security. In the beggining of the series, Worf is mainly known for offering up extremely rash advice and having it completely rejected by Picard. As the series continues, he becomes much more effective.

Geordi La Forge

Geordi is the chief engineer of the Enterprise. He is blind, and during the tv series he wears a visor device so that he can "see". In the movies, this is replaced by artificial eyes. Geordi is a technical genius who often works closely with his friend, Data.

Counselor Troi

Troi is half-human, half-Betazoid, which makes her "empathic" (she senses others emotions). She has the dual job of advising Picard on the bridge and also serving as the ship's psychologist.

Doctor Crusher

Long-time friend of Picard's, her husband had died while serving with Picard. She has one son, Wesley, who serves on the ship for a good deal of the series.
